## Highlights

- **Takapuna Beach** – Swim, paddleboard, or grab gelato.
- **Devonport** – Historic charm and ferry access.
- **Long Bay Regional Park** – Great for picnics and coastal walks.
- **Lake Pupuke** – A volcanic lake with rowing and kayaking.

---

## Tips

- Take the ferry to **Devonport** for a scenic commute.
- Visit **The Commons** or **Jam Café** for brunch.
- Explore **North Head** for tunnels and views.
