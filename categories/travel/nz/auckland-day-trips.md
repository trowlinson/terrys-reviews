## Top Picks

- **Waiheke Island** – Beaches, vineyards, and laid-back charm.
- **Matakana** – Farmers markets, sculpture trails, and boutique wineries.
- **Piha Beach** – Black sand surf and dramatic cliffs.
- **Hobbiton & Waitomo Caves** – Movie magic and glowworm wonder.

---

## Tips

- Book Waiheke ferry tickets in advance during weekends.
- Matakana is best on Saturdays for the market.
- Waitomo + Hobbiton combo tours leave early — plan ahead!
