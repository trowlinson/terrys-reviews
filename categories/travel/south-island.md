## Highlights

- **Queenstown** – Adventure capital with bungy jumping, skiing, and lake cruises.  
- **Milford Sound** – Breathtaking fjord with waterfalls and wildlife.  
- **Lake Tekapo** – Famous turquoise waters and star‑gazing at the Dark Sky Reserve.  
- **Abel Tasman National Park** – Golden beaches and coastal hiking tracks.  
- **Franz Josef Glacier** – Walks and helicopter tours to see the ice up close.  

---

## Tips

- Take a scenic drive along **State Highway 6** for coastal and mountain views.  
- Visit **Fergburger** in Queenstown for iconic eats.  
- Pack layers — South Island weather can change quickly, even in summer.  
- Book cruises in **Milford Sound** early, as spots fill fast.  
- Explore smaller towns like **Wanaka** and **Akaroa** for a more relaxed vibe.
