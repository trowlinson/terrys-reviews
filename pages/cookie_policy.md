# Cookie Policy

<p class="last-updated">Last updated: 22 November 2025</p>

<section class="section" id="what-are-cookies">
    <h2>What are cookies?</h2>
    <p>
    Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site
    function properly, remember your preferences, and provide insights into how visitors use the site.
    </p>
</section>

<section class="section" id="how-we-use-cookies">
    <h2>How we use cookies</h2>
    <ul>
    <li><strong>Essential cookies:</strong> Required for core functionality such as navigation and security.</li>
    <li><strong>Performance cookies:</strong> Help us understand traffic and improve content.</li>
    <li><strong>Functional cookies:</strong> Remember your preferences and settings.</li>
    <li><strong>Advertising cookies:</strong> Support personalised ads and measure their effectiveness (only with your consent).</li>
    </ul>
</section>

<section class="section" id="third-parties">
    <h2>Third-party cookies</h2>
    <p>
    Some cookies are set by third parties (e.g., analytics providers, advertising networks). These parties
    may process data for their own purposes subject to their privacy policies.
    </p>
</section>
